SAMPE Composite Beam Competition>

We participate in the SAMPE student beam competition, designing, building, and testing composite beams made out of carbon, glass, and natural fibers. Last year, at the competition in Long Beach, CA, our glass fiber I-beam teams hit design load of 7000lbs and placed 4th and 5th out of 14 teams!
This year, we will have seven teams competing in May in North Carolina.
